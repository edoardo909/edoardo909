Long Beach - Mauritius

Opened in 2011, Long Beach is the new urban beach resort of Sun Resorts collection,
characterized by a concept that has successfully combined the cheerful buzz of the city and the relaxed lifestyle of the beacha.

An image that immediately arises from its stylistic blend of the urban feel of the ocean and the atmosphere naturally,
which offers the carefree lifestyle of the square contextualized in a spectacular beach 700 meters long and 40 wide,
the longest and larger than the white sandy coastline of any hotel complex currently present in Mauritius.
Adjacent to the lagoon, protected by a constellation of rare coral reefs, home to a rich marine fauna that will satisfy lovers of snorkeling and water sports.

A modern resort that offers tailored services for families thanks to the 255 rooms, all with sea views and specially designed to accommodate many nuclei.
Children and young people will have daytime and evening entertainment, a disco on the beach and an extensive program of excursions,
all offered by the Club Kids and Teens @ Sungeneration.

Long Beach has a meeting space of 300 square meters in 3 parts with modular seating up to 200 guests, including modern audio / video,
and the collaboration of experts in the organization of team-building events.

Long Beach is an elegant and welcoming haven for a holiday that satisfies the aspirations of the consumer trendy and sophisticated,
thanks to its exceptional location, its unique blend of modern architecture and tropical, lively atmosphere with outstanding service
and recreational facilities to which add a series of high-quality dining experiences .
