La Pirogue - Mauritius

La Pirogue is a charming 4-star resort that takes its name from the original local fishing boat called "Pirogue".
A high quality hotel in Mauritian style that stands on the west coast of Mauritius and overlooking a pristine beach surrounded by 35 acres of tropical gardens,
manicured lawns which are combined with plantations of palm trees and lush vegetation .

La Pirogue offers 246 standard and superior cottages and 2 Royal Suites, all characterized by large and spacious, thatched roofs and volcanic rock,
where they housed the British and Dutch royal families, as well as several heads of state and local government memberse.

Wide and varied culinary proposal of the many theme restaurants that will satisfy all tastes, as well as three bars will be happy to serve refreshing cocktails and tasty snacks.

Sun Kids, Teens Club and a rich entertainment program will entertain young and old filling the wide range of high quality services it offers its customers the canoe.
For groups and incentive travel, and the Sesame Muscade Room are available for conferences and business meetings.
