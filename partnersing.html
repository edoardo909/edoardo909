Tourism Queensland

We were appointed as their Public Relations consultants in Italy on a two year contract. As press office, we organised press trips and also generate press releases in Italian to the trade and consumer media.

Cancun Conventions & Visitors Bureau

We represented them on a short-term basis with a specific target : to promote throughout Italy the image of Cancun. We also generated press releases in Italian to the travel and consumer media. Our brief also included: Researching the Italian travel trade market; increasing a data base of travel agents. Specific joint promotion plans done with tour operators.

Gulf Air :

A world-class international airline with links between east and west, utilising the four Gulf hubs of Abu Dhabi, Bahrain, Oman and Qatar. We were appointed as their Public Relations consultants in Italy for 16 months until they closed their Italian route. As their press office, we also generated press releases in Italian to the trade and consumer media and organised press trips. 

 

Victoria Cruises:

A USA company based in New York who offers a selection of luxury cruises on the Eastern Yangtze, in China. We are appointed as their representatives in Italy on an agreed short term ad hoc basis. For the first year during the launch of their product we agreed to do: regular sales contacts with tour operators, incentive houses, travel agents and related airlines. We also participated to promotions, trade shows, seminars, etc. on their behalf. As their Public Relations Consultants for Italy we also generated press releases in Italian to the trade and consumer media and organised press trips.
