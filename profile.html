Destinations Tourism Marketing Consultants Srl, was formed during 1996 and is registered under Italian law. The company has an appropriate legal status to act as representative.

It's directors are as follows:
Mr Ferdinando Parello
Mrs Concetta Rossetti

Destinations is a Milan based integrated marketing services organisation providing sales, marketing and communication skills and expertise to meet clients' specific business needs.

Destinations acts as an information, marketing and sales office for overseas clients seeking to develop their business interests in Italy with emphasis on travel and tourism.

Destinations can establish and develop a separate identity (office name, stationery, telephone, fax lines and dedicated staff) and provide a range of sales and marketing services structured to meet the client's needs and budget.

Additional resources, such as advertising, marketing and public relations services are available if required to support the core activity. Over the years, Destinations' formula has been refined and adapted to suit a number of different client situations ensuring the highest levels of achievement and cost efficiency on a one to one basis.
